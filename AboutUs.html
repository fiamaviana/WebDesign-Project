About US

Hello folks, welcome to JJF tours. We are glad to have you here.

We are three friends that have a passion for travel as a common point and, in 2020, after exploring Dublin, we realized that it was possible to mix our interests and create amazing tours options to visit Dublin.

Combining our passions, we created JJF tours to offer to our public tours to places that were previously visited by us. We have had the opportunity to know new places and we are continually learning about the local history. Now we want to share with you and make your day unforgettable.

Our tours are carefully prepared, with modern and good structure to travel, which include comfortable and luxurious bus, experienced guides and personalized tours with small groups.

We believe that travel is a journey through diversity, culture and history. Our mission is to share with you our passion for traveling and providing tours that will give a real and deep experience about the place that you chose to visit.

Let us show you who we are:

Fiama Marques, 27 years old, from Brazil, living in Ireland since October of 2018 and graduated in business. When she arrived in Dublin, she immediately fell in love with the autumn season. The beautiful yellow leaves fascinated her as she used to walk around traditional neighborhoods while making new friends and learning about the local culture.

Joselia Reis, 30 years, from Brazil, living in Ireland since September 2019 and graduated as a lawyer. Her passion is exploring nature in Dublin. In her free time, she likes to visit parks or go for long walks on trails or around the beach. Ticknock is her favourite place in Dublin.


Come with us and let us show you the beautiful places that Dublin reserves to you.
